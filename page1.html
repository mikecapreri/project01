Top 10 Greatest Fruits:

#1 Oranges:

The orange is the top fruit on this list, because of it's juicy citrus flavors, mixed with the entertainement of peeling and eating the fruit. It is a truly a high quality fruit that provides so much enjoyment to one's everyday life.

#2 Pineapples:

The pineapple is an extremely flavorful fruit. Its high quality flavor is good enough to be first place but the preparation holds it back.

#3 Grapes:

Grapes are incredibly fun to eat as well as having some of the most fruitful flavors.

#4 Strawberries:

The strawberry had to make the top 5 as it is one of the juiciest fruits as well as being small and easy to eat on the go.

#5 Cherries:

The cherry is a great fruit. The fun of trying to tie a knot in the stem using only your mouth provides a sense of novelty to the experience.

#6 Apples:

An apple is a classic as it is one of the most present fruit. It is a staple in the fruit community

#7 Blueberries:

Blueberries are a wonderful fruit. The flavor is outstanding and is enjoyable if you want to eat million of something

#8 Mangos:

I believe the mango is one of the most underrated fruits. With great flavor it should make any top 10, however, it is not as often seen.

#9 Peaches:

The peach is a cool fruit. It’s fuzzy exterior makes it seem welcoming and friendly.

#10 Pears:

The pear is in the bottom spot, because honestly it’s a relatively boring fruit, but still worthy of the top 10.
